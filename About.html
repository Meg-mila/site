About me Projects

Hi there! I’m Margarita Perepecho a UX/UI Designer based in London, UK.

I’ve studied as a front-end developer for almost a year as a full-time student, but decided to jump deeper into UX/UI design. Why? Just because I'm passionate about creating amazing products for others, making their lives easier, happier and brighter.

I like to learn something new every day. I enjoy practicing a user-centred design process, doing user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and creating clean and aesthetic designs. Also, my education as a front-end developer helped me understand how to create a design correctly and how I can transfer it to other developers.

I love travelling, hiking, camping, and taking photos. When I'm not online, you'll find me at the campsite by the campfire, playing the ukulele and singing songs.

Feel free to contact me!
