Let me tell you about myself

In the beginning, I decided to become a front-end developer because I enjoy creating websites. Website creation for me was incredible and fascinating to the point where I even made my first landing pages and one of them is currently live. I then realised that web design for me is easy to understand and is the most important thing for me, as well as it being an interesting and practical experience.

I've spent a year of my education as a front-end developer. This was hard work as we created minigames, responsive web sites, and used JS. For this reason I now know how to create websites. On the other hand, my education showed me that I can obtain information independently and learn it.

This is how I started to study UX / UI design. I have completed the online course "Google UX Design" and continue to improve my web design skills and put them into practice on my own. I publish my projects on behance and create simple and user-friendly designs. My education as a front-end developer helped me understand how to create a design correctly and how I can transfer it to other developers. I really like what I am doing now and it motivates me a lot.
