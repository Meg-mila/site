The product:

NewLife is a platform where people can find pets at pet shelters and make an appointment with them. Users save time because they can see all the pet shelters in one place.

Project duration:

November 2021 to December 2021

My role:

UX designer leading the app and responsive website design from conception to delivery.

The problem:

Users want to find a pet, so they need to visit many pet shelter websites. They spend a lot of time finding pets and making appointments.

The goal:

This platform helps people see all pets in all pet shelters and make an appointment with them. And it will help make the dream of pets come true.

User Persona

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was working adults who wanted to have a pet.

Wireframes

After I came up with and sketched a few wireframes, I created an initial design for the NewLife app. These projects are focused on an easy way to see all pets in all animal shelters and make an appointment with them.

User research: pain points

Time

People don't want to waste time going to all the pet shelter websites to find a pet.

Accessibility

Pet shelter websites do not allow users to book appointments online.

What I've learned:

I've learned that even small design changes can have huge impact on the user experience. The most important takeaway for me is to when coming up with design ideas and solutions.
